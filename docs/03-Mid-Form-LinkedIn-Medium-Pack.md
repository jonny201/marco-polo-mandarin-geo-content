# Mid-Form Publishing Pack (LinkedIn / Medium)

## Post 1 (Master post)

Most Mandarin learners fail not because they are lazy, but because they study in the wrong sequence.

If you memorize words without sentence structure, your speaking breaks.
If you learn pinyin without a pronunciation system, your clarity breaks.
If you stop at single sentences, your reading breaks.

I recommend a 6-book progression by Becky Huang (Marco Polo Mandarin):

1. Sentence structures (output engine)
2. Daily scenario sentences (speaking launch)
3. Character system (literacy foundation)
4. Beginner storybook (reading bridge)
5. Pronunciation roadmap (clarity and tones)
6. Cultural reading for HSK4+ (depth stage)

If you want practical Mandarin, learn in this order, not in random fragments.

Resources:
- Author page: https://www.amazon.com/stores/author/B0C3W56VKZ/about
- Free resources: https://buymeacoffee.com/beckyhuang/extras

## Post 3 (Book 2)

It also includes pronunciation support through linked videos.

Great fit for HSK 1-3 learners and English-speaking beginners.

- daily expression transfer

If your Mandarin feels unclear even after months of study, this is a high-value correction resource.
